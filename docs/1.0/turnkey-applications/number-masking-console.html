Number Masking Console

Overview

Telestax Number Masking ensures that customers and businesses can easily make calls and send messages without exposing personal phone numbers. It offers many advantages to both businesses and users alike.

With Telestax, Communications Providers can develop a turnkey phone number masking application in minutes leveraging a comprehensive Voice and SMS API. Using the Number Masking Console, a Communications Provider can create, manage, and view the accounts of their Enterprise customers, as well as any users.

Both the Communications Provider Admin and Enterprise Admin can create, manage, and view Applications, Contexts, and Masked Pools. To learn more about managing Number Masking features, please view the Business Customer Console Doc Getting Started with Enterprise Number Masking Console Guide.

This document covers the features and functionalities of the Number Masking Console (UI) from a customer or user point of view.

To learn more about the API, please refer to the Getting Started with the Number Masking API Guide.

To sign in to the Number Masking Console please navigate to https://<your domain>/numbermaskingconsole/.

Terminology

As part of this documentation, the following terms will be used to describe specific individuals, concepts, and organizations.

Communications Provider

Communications Provider generally refers to any organization providing communications products, applications, capabilities, or services. The term can refer to operators, carriers, providers, suppliers, or any other entity that delivers communications capabilities to enterprise or business customers.

Business Customer

An enterprise or business customer that relies on a Communications Provider to deliver communications products, capabilities, or services to customers or end users.

Business Customer

Communications Providers serve Business Customers. Through their own Accounts, Communications Providers can create, manage, or delete Business Customer Sub Accounts. By setting up Sub Accounts for Business Customers, the Communications Provider can easily manage billing and invoices for a specific Business Customer.

Business Customer List

Users can view a list of all Business Customers that have been added under their organization. Business Customer’s account can be filtered based on name.

Business Customer Details

Users can view details of a particular Business by clicking any Name in the list. Users can delete the Business Customer by clicking on the “Delete Business Customer” button. Users can also update the Business Customer and adjust the configuration as explained below.

Concurrent Sessions: A masked number can be used simultaneously in multiple sessions or contexts. The minimum value can be 1. If a Business Customer tries to use a masked number in more than the value of the “concurrentMaskNumberLimit,” the Application will display Error Code 474 “Masked Numbers are not available”.

Add Business Customer

Whenever a Communications Provider adds a new Sub Account for a Business Customer, a mandatory User must be defined. This User gets created under Restcomm as a Sub Account of the Communications Provider Account. The Communications Provider should provision all the phone numbers under this Sub Account in Restcomm as explained in the documentation located here. These phone numbers are available to Business Customers to create a Masked Number Pool for any Application.

It is expected that the Communications Provider will not share newly created User credentials with Business Customers. The Communications Provider should create more Users via the User Management API and only share newly created credentials. This is because the first User created with a Business Customer will have all the Phone Numbers provisioned and the Communications Provider would not want Business Customers to make any changes to those numbers.

Follow the steps below to add an enterprise:

  • Click on the “Add Business Customer” button in the Business Customer list page .

  • Enter the name of the new Business Customer.

  • Choose an Administrator for this Business Customer/Sub Account. Enter the Admin Email Address. All phone numbers will be provisioned under this Sub Account.

  • Enter the password for the Admin account. This will be set in Restcomm.

  • Enter a friendly name for the Admin User.

Enterprise name and Email ID must be unique.

Password should meet the following criteria:

  • At least one Uppercase letter(A,B,C,D……​)

  • At least one Lowercase letter(a,b,c,d,…​…​.)

  • At least one number(1,2,3,4,5….)

  • At least one special character(!,@,#,$,%,&,…​…​.)

  • Length of the password should be greater than equal to 8.

Once the Business Customer is created, click on the “Applications” link of a particular enterprise to see all other options mentioned as described in the Business Customer Console documentation.

Search

All search options are outlined in Business Customer Console Docs with the exception of the Business Customer search feature which is only available in the Communications Provider Admin Console. As part of this feature the Communications Provider Admin can search Enterprise accounts by selecting the Display Name as a filter.
