Programmable SMS

Overview

Get a complete tool kit that includes powerful APIs and our easy to use Visual Designer system. Programmatically send and receive SMS globally for your messaging communications.

Programmable SMS features

Two Ways Messaging

Virtual numbers reachable via SMS or Chat VoIP SIP Clients allow your customers to reply to messages for an interactive, chat-like experience.

Smart Encoding

Engage customers in their native tongue. Intelligent automatic unicode translation to ensure your emojis and non-standard characters make it to their destination the way you intended.

Delivery reports

Track delivery rates and performance of your SMS campaign. Receive real-time Delivery Reports of SMS messages sent.

Bring Your Own Network (BYON)

Bring Your Own Network (BYON) is a way to keep your existing phone provider for incoming or outgoing calls. CPaaS works with any SMPP or SS7-capable carrier and gives you the flexibility and control to choose the right phone company with the right plan for you. If you dont already have a carrier for your SMS/MMS, then we have exclusive partnerships with Carriers that are guaranteed to save you lots on your phone bill

Intelligent SMS splitting

We support sending messages longer than 160 characters and automatically split them with no additional effort on the part of the user based on the encoding. No more lost messages or ugly, manual chunking.

Real-time SMS notifications and logs

Fetch your SMS logs in real-time with our APIs. Receive detailed information including message direction, message status, and message time stamps

Start easily with CPaaS Visual Designer

Create SMS applications using a drag and drop interface. CPaaS Visual Designer lets you enrich your application with custom code on your favorite programming language.

You can start building your application from scratch or use one of the many templates available. Sign in to CPaaS console and start quickly using the template gallery.
